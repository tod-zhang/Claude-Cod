## General Knowledge (No Citation Needed)

- Brown glass provides UV protection for beer bottles
- Crown caps are the standard closure for beer bottles
- Double seaming is the standard method for sealing aluminum cans
- Depalletizers unload containers from pallets onto conveyors
- Labeling and date coding are standard packaging line steps
- Case packing groups containers for distribution
- "Skunking" is the common term for light-struck beer flavor
