## General Knowledge (No Citation Needed)

- Mayonnaise is a high-viscosity emulsion product
- Piston fillers use positive displacement technology
- ROI formula: (Net benefit / Total cost) x 100
- Payback period formula: Total cost / Monthly benefit
- Production tiers (small batch, medium, commercial, industrial) are standard industry classifications
- BPM calculation formula (Fill Heads x Cycles Per Minute) is standard engineering calculation
- CIP (clean-in-place) is standard food industry terminology
- Container size examples (450ml, 2.5L) are common retail/foodservice formats
