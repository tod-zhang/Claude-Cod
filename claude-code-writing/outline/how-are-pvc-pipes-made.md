# How Are PVC Pipes Made

## Article Strategy

**Core Thesis:** PVC pipe manufacturing is a precisely orchestrated process where temperature control, chemical additives, and mechanical precision must work together - understanding each station reveals why PVC pipes are both ubiquitous and reliable.

**Unique Angle:** Factory tour narrative - taking readers inside a PVC pipe factory, revealing the production process step by step

**Hook Strategy:** Surprising-stat - lead with the counter-intuitive fact that PVC doesn't melt like other plastics

**Reader Transformation:**
- FROM: Knows PVC pipes exist everywhere but has no idea how they transform from raw materials into finished pipes
- TO: Can describe the basic manufacturing steps (mixing, extrusion, cooling, cutting) and visualize what happens inside a PVC pipe factory

**Opinion Stances:**
1. Twin screw extruders are the better choice for PVC - recommend over single screw due to thermal sensitivity
2. The compounding/additives step is the most underappreciated part of the process - it literally prevents complete failure

**Frameworks:**
- Introduction: Direct Hook (surprising stat about fusion)
- Conclusion: Next Journey Step

- The extruder as the heart of the factory

### H2: How Do Manufacturers Control Pipe Size and Shape?
- Vacuum sizing/calibration
- Differential air pressure maintains shape
- PVC draws down 15-20%
