# HDPE vs PVC: How to Choose the Right Pipe Material for Your Project

## Article Strategy

**Core Thesis:** The choice between HDPE and PVC isn't simply about cost - it's about matching material properties to your specific application requirements. While PVC offers lower upfront costs and rigidity for simple residential applications, HDPE's flexibility, fusion-welded joints, and dramatically lower failure rates make it the superior choice for demanding installations where long-term reliability matters.

**Unique Angle:** Project material selection guide - focus on decision-making framework rather than generic property lists
