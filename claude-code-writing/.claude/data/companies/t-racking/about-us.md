# T Racking

## Company Overview

T Racking is a leading warehouse racking and shelving system manufacturer based in China, serving 8,300+ customers worldwide. The company specializes in industrial storage solutions with a focus on quality, customization, and fast delivery.

## Products & Services

### Core Products
- **Pallet Racking Systems**: Selective, drive-in, push-back, shuttle systems
- **Industrial Shelving**: Longspan, boltless, angle, mobile shelving
- **Automated Storage**: AS/RS (Automated Storage and Retrieval Systems)
- **Steel Profiles & Components**: Structural components for racking
- **Ikuby Carports**: Authorized manufacturer

### Services
- Free site design consultation
- Professional assembly and installation
- Warehouse management software integration (WCS/WMS)
- Mix loading services
- Maintenance inspection

## Manufacturing Capabilities

- **Facility Size**: 200,000+ square meters
- **Material Stock**: 10,000+ tons ready inventory
- **Equipment**: Automated welding robots, CNC machines, powder coating line
- **Design Team**: 30+ engineers with average 10 years experience

## Key Value Propositions

1. **Lifetime Warranty** on all products
2. **Fast Delivery**: 15 days after order confirmation
3. **Low MOQ**: Single-piece customization available
4. **Competitive Pricing**: Large-scale manufacturing advantage
5. **Free Samples**: Available for quality verification

## Standards & Certifications

- Australia AS4084
