## FAQ

<details>
<summary>What information do you need to quote a flywheel?</summary>
Diameter, thickness, weight (if known), mounting pattern, material preference, balance requirements, and quantity.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can you machine ring gear seats and pilot bores?</summary>
Yes. We provide complete machining including ring gear seats, pilot bores, mounting bolt patterns, and critical surfaces.
</details>

<details>
<summary>What balance grades can you achieve?</summary>
Standard is ISO 1940 G6.3. For precision applications, G2.5 or tighter is available with dynamic balancing.
</details>
