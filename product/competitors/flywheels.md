## 5. Differentiation Strategy

### Must-Have Differentiators

1. **Clear Specifications**: Diameter range, weight range, tolerances
2. **Material Options with Grades**: Gray iron vs ductile with ASTM specs
3. **Balance Grade Options**: ISO 1940 balance grades explained

### Unique Angle

> Competitors focus on either aftermarket replacement OR large custom machining. We offer complete casting + machining for custom OEM flywheels with clear specifications and material traceability.
