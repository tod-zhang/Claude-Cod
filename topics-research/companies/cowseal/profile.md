# Company Profile: Cowseal

## Basic Information
- **Company Name**: Cowseal (牛封)
- **Website**: https://cowseal.com
- **Industry**: Mechanical Seal Manufacturing (机械密封制造)
- **Location**: Ningbo, China
- **Target Market**: Global B2B

## Products & Services

### Main Products
- **Mechanical Seals**: Type 1, Type 21, Grundfos, Burgmann series (MG1, MG12, MG13, MGS20)
- **Pump Seals**: For water pumps, centrifugal pumps, chemical pumps
- **Cartridge Seals**: Pre-assembled units for easy installation
- **Custom Seals**: Made-to-order sealing solutions

### Key Materials
- Silicon Carbide (碳化硅)
- Carbon Graphite (碳石墨)
- Tungsten Carbide (碳化钨)
- Ceramic (陶瓷)
- Stainless Steel (不锈钢)
