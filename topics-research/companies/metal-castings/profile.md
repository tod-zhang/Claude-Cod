# Company Profile: KT Metal Castings

## Basic Information
- **Company Name**: KT Metal Casting Co., Ltd.
- **Website**: https://metal-castings.com
- **Industry**: Metal Casting (Sand Casting & Investment Casting)
- **Location**: Weifang City, Shandong Province, China
- **Founded**: November 2015 (Own factory since 2018)
- **Target Market**: Global (North America, Europe, Australia, Southeast Asia)

## Products & Services

### Main Products
- **Counterweights**: Heavy-duty balancing components for industrial equipment
- **Flywheels & Flywheel Housings**: Energy storage and torque management
- **Motor Housings**: Protective enclosures for electric motors
- **Valve Castings**: Flow control components
- **Snatch Blocks**: Lifting and rigging components
- **Handwheels & Manifolds**: Control and distribution components
- **Custom Steel & Iron Castings**: OEM parts to customer specifications

### Casting Processes
- **Sand Casting**: Complex geometries, medium to large-scale production
- **Investment Casting (Lost Wax)**: Precision parts with tight tolerances, near-net-shape
- **CNC Machining**: Secondary machining for finished parts

### Materials
- Steel (carbon steel, alloy steel, stainless steel)
- Iron (gray iron, ductile iron)
- Aluminum
- Brass

### Services Offered
- Pattern making and mold development
- Casting production (prototype to high-volume)
- CNC machining and finishing
- Quality inspection and testing
