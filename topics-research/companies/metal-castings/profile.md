## Products & Services

### Main Products
- **Counterweights**: Heavy-duty balancing components for industrial equipment
- **Flywheels & Flywheel Housings**: Energy storage and torque management for automotive and industrial machinery
- **Motor Housings**: Protective enclosures for electric motors
- **Valve Castings**: Flow control components for various industries
- **Snatch Blocks**: Lifting and rigging components
- **Handwheels & Manifolds**: Control and distribution components
- **Custom Steel & Iron Castings**: OEM parts to customer specifications

### Services Offered
- **Sand Casting**: Durable, cost-effective components for medium to large-scale production
- **Investment Casting (Lost Wax)**: Precision casting with tight tolerances and near-net-shape components
- **CNC Machining**: Secondary machining services for finished parts
- **Quality Control**: Comprehensive inspection and testing

### Manufacturing Capabilities
- **Sand Casting**: Complex geometries, various alloys, prototype to high-volume production
- **Investment Casting**: Intricate details, thin-walled sections, superior surface finish
- **Materials**: Steel, iron, aluminum, brass, specialty alloys
- **Finishing**: Near-net-shape components reducing secondary machining needs
