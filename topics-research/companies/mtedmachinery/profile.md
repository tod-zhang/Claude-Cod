- **Company Name**: MTED Machinery
- **Website**: https://mtedmachinery.com
- **Industry**: Aluminum Foil Container Making Machinery

## Products & Services

### Main Products
- **Aluminum Foil Container Making Machines**: Complete production lines from raw foil to finished stacking
- **C Series Machines**: Compact, space-efficient design for smaller operations
- **H Series Machines**: Maximum throughput for high-volume production

### Services Offered
- **Custom Mold Development**: Tooling designed to client specifications
- **Technical Support**: Ongoing engineering support
- **Machine Configuration**: Tailored solutions for specific production needs

### Manufacturing Capabilities
- Stamping, trimming, and stacking automation
- Precision die systems
- Programmable controls
- Quick-changeover capability (minimal production interruption)
- 20+ mold designs available
